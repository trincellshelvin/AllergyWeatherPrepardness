About the Designer

This journey has been a long one with learning new ways and tools to design any website or app. I originally started this journey in 1999 while in school for Business Information Systems where I had an emphasis in Web Design.

This preparedness app is a labor of pride for me as I am also an allergy sufferer with an underlying condition that can be triggered due to different indoor and outdoor environments. It will serve as a list to get prepared for the weather event and a place to look up data on Air Quality Index with Health Tips and Trivia Games for those down times during the event.
